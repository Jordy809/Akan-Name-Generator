Akan Name Generator: Find Your Ghanaian Name

Ever wondered what your Akan name would be? This fun and easy-to-use web app helps you find out! Just enter your date of birth and select your gender, and the app will calculate the day of the week you were born. Using traditional Ghanaian Akan naming customs, it then reveals the Akan name linked to your birthday making it a simple and enjoyable way to learn about Ghanaian culture.
